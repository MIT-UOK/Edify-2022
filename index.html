Inter University Article Competition

The 18th Issue of the most awaited University Business Magazine is coming soon.
Seize this phenomenal opportunity to publish your ideas on Management, IT or Entrepreneurship on Exposition.

Exposition

Exposition Magazine

Issue 17

With the vision of “A Thought Leader in Digital Transformation” the Department of Industrial Management publishes the Exposition Magazine, the most outstanding magazine of the year to bridge the gap between Management and IT platforms. It is one of the leading university business magazines issued to mark the footprints of undergraduates in the corporate world. The legacy unfolds for the 17th consecutive year and for the first time ever we are ready to publish your valuable articles.

Write away to your heart's content and submit your articles to 'Edify', the Inter-University article competition of Exposition.

The winning article will be published along with other valuable content, such as interviews with renowned personalities and articles on the latest business / IT trends.

Guidelines

Rules And Regulations


•Entrepreneurship, IT and Management are the themes of Exposition 2022. Consider these themes when writing the articles.

•The articles should be written in English.

•Article word count should be approximately 1000-1500 words.

•Articles should be written as a continuous narrative in a chapter or article style, not as a list of points.

•The structure of the content should be relatively formal.

•Articles should be well researched and therefore should cover unique areas.

Important


•Plagiarism is prohibited. Reproduced information from the web is not accepted.

•Articles should be based on your original ideas and views, and should not contain any phrases directly from the web.

•Facts and claims must be verifiable.

•Articles should represent viewpoints fairly and without bias while focusing on the main topic.

•Topic should be relevant and creative.

•When choosing the winning article, the decision of the judges will be final.

Submission Guidelines


•Articles should be illustrated with appropriate & high resolution (300 dpi) images.

•Avoid Clip Arts at all purposes. Use graphs, charts & other graphical tools. If graphs or charts are used, the name should contain the purpose and what it shows.

•Article submission link will be provided after the registrations.
